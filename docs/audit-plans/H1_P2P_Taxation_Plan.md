# H1 REVIEW - PROCURE-TO-PAY & TAXATION AUDIT PLAN
## SNVA TravelTech Pvt. Ltd. | FY2025-26

**Audit Period**: April - September 2025  
**Fieldwork Period**: October - November 2025  
**Lead Auditor**: Team Member 2 (TM2)  
**Support**: Team Member 3 (TM3) - Data Analytics

## PART A: PROCURE-TO-PAY & VENDOR MANAGEMENT

### OBJECTIVES

1. Evaluate P2P cycle from requisition to payment
2. Ensure robust vendor onboarding and KYC controls
3. Verify purchase approvals and budget compliance
4. Test 3-way match (PO, GRN, Invoice) controls
5. Validate payment authorization and disbursement controls
6. Assess vendor master data integrity

### SCOPE

**In-Scope Processes:**
1. Vendor selection and onboarding
2. Purchase requisition and approval
3. Purchase order issuance
4. Goods/services receipt and verification (GRN)
5. Invoice verification and recording
6. Payment processing and authorization
7. Vendor master data maintenance
8. Accounts payable management

**Key Expense Categories:**
- IT services and software
- Office supplies and equipment
- Professional fees (legal, audit, consulting)
- Rent and utilities
- Travel and conveyance
- Marketing and advertising
- Other operational expenses

### RISK ASSESSMENT

**High Risk Areas:**
- New vendor onboarding (KYC gaps, fraud risk)
- High-value purchases (>₹1 lakh) - authorization risk
- Vendor advances - misappropriation risk
- Vendor master changes (bank account) - fraud risk
- Sole-sourcing without justification - value for money risk

**Medium Risk Areas:**
- Purchase approval compliance
- 3-way match execution
- Payment authorization matrix adherence
- AP aging and timely payments

**Low Risk Areas:**
- Routine low-value purchases
- Repeat vendor transactions

### METHODOLOGY

#### Phase 1: Process Understanding (Week 1)

**Activities:**
1. Review P2P SOP in detail
2. Conduct walkthrough with:
   - Admin Head (procurement, vendor onboarding)
   - Finance Executive (invoice recording, payments)
   - Finance Head (approvals)
3. Understand vendor onboarding process
4. Review vendor selection policy
5. Understand payment authorization matrix
6. Identify key controls

**Deliverables:**
- Process understanding memo
- Control identification matrix
- Risk assessment worksheet

#### Phase 2: Data Collection (Week 2)

**Data Requests:**
1. Purchase register from Tally (Apr-Sep 2025) - TM3 to extract
2. List of all vendors with transaction values
3. List of new vendors added in H1
4. Vendor master file (KYC documents)
5. Sample of purchase orders
6. Sample of GRNs
7. Bank statements for payment verification
8. AP aging report as of Sept 30, 2025
9. List of vendor advances (if any)
10. Budget/AOP for expense categories

**TM3 Support:**
- Extract purchase register from Tally
- Identify high-value purchases
- Scan for anomalies (round amounts, weekend entries)
- Prepare expense analysis by category

#### Phase 3: Control Testing (Week 3-5)

#### Test 1: Vendor Onboarding and KYC
**Objective**: Ensure proper vendor onboarding with complete KYC

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain list of all new vendors in H1
2. Test 100% if <10 vendors, else sample 10
3. For each vendor:
   - Verify Vendor Registration Form completed
   - Check KYC documents:
     * PAN card copy
     * GST registration certificate
     * Bank account details (cancelled cheque)
     * Address proof
     * Any other required documents
   - Verify Admin Head approval
   - Check vendor created in Tally with correct details
   - Verify first payment made to verified bank account

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-01

**Expected Evidence:**
- Complete vendor file with all KYC documents
- Admin Head approval email/sign-off
- Tally vendor master matching KYC details

#### Test 2: Purchase Requisition and Approval
**Objective**: Verify purchases are requisitioned and approved per policy

**Procedure:**
1. Select sample of 25 purchase transactions:
   - Stratify by value (high, medium, low)
   - Cover all expense categories
   - Ensure all 6 months covered
2. For each purchase:
   - Verify purchase requisition exists
   - Check departmental approval
   - Verify budget/AOP comparison
   - Check Admin Head approval (if required per threshold)
   - Verify approval before PO issuance

**Sample Selection:**
- All purchases >₹1 lakh (if <10, else top 10)
- Random selection for remaining to reach 25
- Include at least one from each major expense category

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-02 to H1-P2P-04

**Expected Evidence:**
- Purchase requisition form
- Departmental approval
- Budget comparison worksheet
- Admin Head approval (for high-value)

#### Test 3: Competitive Bidding
**Objective**: Ensure competitive bidding for significant purchases

**Procedure:**
1. Identify all purchases >₹50,000 in H1
2. Test all if <10, else sample 10
3. For each:
   - Verify multiple quotations obtained (minimum 3)
   - Check quotation comparison sheet
   - Verify selection rationale documented
   - Check approval for sole-sourcing (if applicable)

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-05

**Expected Evidence:**
- Quotations from multiple vendors
- Comparison sheet with selection rationale
- Approval for any sole-sourcing exceptions

#### Test 4: Goods Receipt and Verification (GRN)
**Objective**: Ensure goods/services received are verified against PO

**Procedure:**
1. For the same 25 purchase samples (Test 2):
   - Verify GRN prepared
   - Check GRN approval
   - Verify quantity/quality matches PO
   - Check for any discrepancies noted
   - Verify discrepancy resolution (debit note, etc.)

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-06

**Expected Evidence:**
- GRN document
- Approval sign-off
- Match with PO
- Discrepancy resolution documentation (if any)

#### Test 5: Invoice Verification and 3-Way Match
**Objective**: Verify invoices matched with PO and GRN before recording

**Procedure:**
1. For the same 25 purchase samples:
   - Verify vendor invoice received
   - Check 3-way match performed:
     * Invoice vs PO (price, terms)
     * Invoice vs GRN (quantity)
     * Invoice calculations correct
   - Verify Finance team approval before recording
   - Check invoice recorded in Tally correctly
   - Verify GST input credit claimed appropriately

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-07 to H1-P2P-08

**Expected Evidence:**
- Vendor invoice
- 3-way match documentation
- Finance approval
- Tally entry matching invoice

#### Test 6: Payment Authorization and Disbursement
**Objective**: Ensure payments follow authorization matrix

**Procedure:**
1. Select sample of 20 vendor payments:
   - Include high-value payments (>₹5 lakhs)
   - Mix of payment methods (NEFT, RTGS, cheque)
   - Cover all 6 months
2. For each payment:
   - Verify supporting documents (invoice, PO, GRN)
   - Check Finance Head initiation
   - Verify dual approval per bank authorization matrix
   - Trace payment to bank statement
   - Verify payment to vendor's registered bank account
   - Check timely payment (per credit terms)

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-09 to H1-P2P-10

**Expected Evidence:**
- Payment summary with supporting documents
- Approval emails/bank portal logs
- Bank statement showing payment
- Match with vendor's KYC bank account

#### Test 7: Vendor Master Data Management
**Objective**: Ensure vendor master changes are controlled

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain log of vendor master changes in H1 (TM3 from Tally)
2. Test all changes if <10, else sample 10
3. For each change:
   - Verify reason for change
   - Check authorization (Admin Head/Finance Head)
   - Verify supporting documentation (new bank details, etc.)
   - Check Tally user who made the change (authorized?)

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-11

**Expected Evidence:**
- Change request form
- Authorization approval
- Supporting documents
- Tally audit trail

#### Test 8: Vendor Advances
**Objective**: Verify vendor advances are per policy and tracked

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain list of vendor advances in H1
2. Test all advances
3. For each:
   - Verify business justification
   - Check approval per policy
   - Verify advance agreement/terms
   - Check subsequent adjustment against invoices
   - Verify any outstanding advances at Sept 30

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-12

**Expected Evidence:**
- Advance request and justification
- Approval per authorization matrix
- Advance agreement
- Subsequent invoice adjustments

#### Test 9: Accounts Payable Aging and Management
**Objective**: Assess AP management effectiveness

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain AP aging as of Sept 30, 2025
2. Analyze aging buckets
3. For overdue payables (>credit terms):
   - Verify Finance Head review
   - Check for disputes or issues
   - Verify subsequent payment (Oct-Nov)
4. Check for any unusual credit balances

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-13

**Expected Evidence:**
- AP aging report
- Finance Head review notes
- Subsequent payment evidence

#### Test 10: Segregation of Duties
**Objective**: Ensure proper segregation in P2P process

**Procedure:**
1. Document roles:
   - Who approves purchases?
   - Who receives goods?
   - Who records invoices?
   - Who initiates payments?
   - Who approves payments?
2. Check for conflicts
3. Verify supervisory oversight

**Workpaper**: H1-P2P-14

**Expected Evidence:**
- Role matrix
- Tally user access rights
- Evidence of reviews

## PART B: TAXATION & STATUTORY COMPLIANCE

### OBJECTIVES

1. Ensure compliance with GST, TDS, and income tax laws
2. Verify tax calculations are accurate
3. Confirm timely tax deposits and return filings
4. Test compliance calendar maintenance
5. Validate tax reconciliations

### SCOPE

**In-Scope Taxes:**
1. **GST**: Output liability, input credit, returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B)
2. **TDS**: Deduction on payments, deposits, returns (26Q, 27Q)
3. **Advance Tax**: Q1 and Q2 payments
4. **Other**: PF, ESI (cursory review for H1)

### RISK ASSESSMENT

**High Risk Areas:**
- Late tax payments (interest and penalties)
- Incorrect TDS rates or thresholds
- GST input credit mismatches (GSTR-2B vs books)
- Missed return filings

**Medium Risk Areas:**
- Tax calculation errors
- Compliance calendar maintenance
- Reconciliation gaps

**Low Risk Areas:**
- Standard tax deductions on routine payments

### METHODOLOGY

#### Phase 1: Process Understanding (Week 1)

**Activities:**
1. Review Taxation SOP and General Compliance SOP
2. Interview:
   - Finance Executive (tax calculations)
   - Senior Accounts Manager (compliance calendar, returns)
   - Finance Manager (reviews, approvals)
3. Understand compliance calendar process
4. Review tax filing schedule

**Deliverables:**
- Process understanding memo
- Compliance calendar review

#### Phase 2: Data Collection (Week 2)

**Data Requests:**
1. Compliance calendar for H1
2. GST returns filed (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) for Apr-Sep
3. GST payment challans
4. GSTR-2B downloads for sample months
5. TDS deduction schedules (monthly)
6. TDS payment challans
7. TDS returns (26Q for Q1, 27Q if applicable)
8. Advance tax computation and payment challans
9. Tally ledgers for GST and TDS accounts

#### Phase 3: Control Testing (Week 3-5)

#### Test 11: Compliance Calendar Maintenance
**Objective**: Verify compliance calendar is maintained and monitored

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain compliance calendar for a sample month (July 2025)
2. Verify it includes all recurring deadlines:
   - GST returns (11th/13th of next month)
   - TDS deposits (7th of next month)
   - PF/ESI deposits (15th of next month)
   - Other statutory deadlines
3. Check tasks marked as completed with dates
4. Verify Finance Manager review

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-01

**Expected Evidence:**
- Compliance calendar document
- Completion tick marks
- Finance Manager review sign-off

#### Test 12: GST Output Liability
**Objective**: Verify GST on sales is correctly calculated and paid

**Procedure:**
1. Select one month (July 2025) for detailed testing
2. Obtain sales register from Tally
3. Recalculate GST output liability
4. Compare with GSTR-1 filed
5. Compare with GSTR-3B filed
6. Verify GST payment challan
7. Check payment made by 20th of next month
8. Verify Senior Accounts Manager review before filing

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-02

**Expected Evidence:**
- Sales register
- GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filed returns
- GST payment challan
- Senior Accounts Manager review notes

#### Test 13: GST Input Credit Reconciliation
**Objective**: Ensure input credit is reconciled with vendor filings

**Procedure:**
1. Select two months (June and August 2025)
2. For each month:
   - Obtain purchase register from Tally
   - Calculate input GST from books
   - Obtain GSTR-2B for the month
   - Compare books vs GSTR-2B
   - Identify mismatches
   - Verify Finance Executive follow-up on mismatches
   - Check if mismatches resolved or carried forward

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-03 to H1-TAX-04

**Expected Evidence:**
- Purchase register
- GSTR-2B download
- Reconciliation worksheet
- Mismatch follow-up documentation

#### Test 14: TDS Deduction on Payments
**Objective**: Verify TDS deducted correctly on applicable payments

**Procedure:**
1. Select 10 payments subject to TDS:
   - Professional fees (194J)
   - Contractor payments (194C)
   - Rent (194I)
   - Salary (if any non-payroll) (192)
   - Mix across H1 period
2. For each payment:
   - Verify TDS applicability and rate
   - Recalculate TDS amount
   - Check TDS deducted in payment summary
   - Verify Finance Head review
   - Check TDS recorded in books
   - Verify subsequent TDS deposit (by 7th of next month)

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-05 to H1-TAX-06

**Expected Evidence:**
- Payment summary with TDS calculation
- Finance Head review
- Tally TDS ledger entry
- TDS payment challan

#### Test 15: TDS Monthly Reconciliation
**Objective**: Ensure TDS deducted reconciles with TDS in books

**Procedure:**
1. Select one month (July 2025)
2. Obtain monthly TDS deduction schedule
3. Obtain Tally TDS payable ledger for the month
4. Reconcile:
   - TDS deducted per schedule
   - TDS recorded in Tally
   - TDS deposited per challan
5. Verify Finance Manager review

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-07

**Expected Evidence:**
- TDS deduction schedule
- Tally ledger
- Reconciliation worksheet
- Finance Manager review

#### Test 16: TDS Returns Filing
**Objective**: Verify TDS returns filed timely and accurately

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain Q1 FY25-26 TDS return (26Q)
2. Verify filing date (due by 31st July 2025)
3. Reconcile return with:
   - Quarterly TDS deduction schedule
   - Tally TDS ledger (Apr-Jun)
   - TDS payment challans (Apr-Jun)
4. Check Senior Accounts Manager review before filing
5. Verify TDS certificates (16A) issued to vendors

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-08

**Expected Evidence:**
- Filed TDS return (26Q)
- Filing acknowledgment
- Reconciliation worksheet
- Senior Accounts Manager review
- Sample Form 16A

#### Test 17: Advance Tax Computation and Payment
**Objective**: Verify advance tax calculated and paid timely

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain advance tax computation for Q1 and Q2
2. Verify computation based on AOP/projected profits
3. Check Finance Head/MD approval
4. Verify Q1 payment (by 15th June 2025)
5. Verify Q2 payment (by 15th September 2025)
6. Check if any true-up done in Q2 based on actuals

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-09

**Expected Evidence:**
- Advance tax computation worksheet
- Approval emails
- Payment challans
- True-up analysis (if any)

#### Test 18: Statutory Dues - PF/ESI (Cursory Review)
**Objective**: Cursory check of PF/ESI payments for H1

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain PF payment challans for Apr-Sep (6 months)
2. Verify payment by 15th of each subsequent month
3. Obtain ESI payment challans (if applicable)
4. Verify payment by 15th of each subsequent month
5. Cross-check with payroll records (high-level)

**Note**: Detailed testing in Q3 audit

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-10

**Expected Evidence:**
- PF challans
- ESI challans
- Payment dates verification

#### Test 19: Bank Authorization Matrix for Tax Payments
**Objective**: Verify tax payments follow authorization matrix

**Procedure:**
1. Select 5 tax payment transactions:
   - GST payment
   - TDS payment
   - Advance tax payment
   - PF payment
   - ESI payment (if applicable)
2. For each:
   - Verify Finance Head authorization
   - Check dual approval if required
   - Trace to bank statement

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-11

**Expected Evidence:**
- Payment approval emails/logs
- Bank statements

#### Test 20: Analytical Procedures
**Objective**: Identify anomalies in tax data

**Procedure (TM3 Support):**
1. Trend analysis:
   - Monthly GST output vs revenue
   - Monthly GST input vs purchases
   - Monthly TDS deducted vs applicable expenses
2. Ratio analysis:
   - Effective GST rate
   - TDS as % of applicable expenses
3. Investigate significant variances

**Workpaper**: H1-TAX-12 (TM3)

**Expected Evidence:**
- Trend charts
- Variance analysis
- Management explanations
