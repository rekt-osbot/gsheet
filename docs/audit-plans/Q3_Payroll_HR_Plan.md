# Q3 REVIEW - PAYROLL & HR COMPLIANCE AUDIT PLAN
## SNVA TravelTech Pvt. Ltd. | FY2025-26

**Audit Period**: October - December 2025  
**Fieldwork Period**: January - February 2026  
**Lead Auditor**: Team Member 3 (TM3)  
**Support**: Team Member 1 (TM1) - Financial Integration | Team Member 2 (TM2) - Compliance

---

## OBJECTIVES

1. Ensure payroll accuracy and proper deductions (PF, ESI, TDS)
2. Verify timely salary disbursement
3. Evaluate employee lifecycle controls (hiring, changes, exits)
4. Confirm statutory HR compliance (PF, ESI, TDS, PT)
5. Validate payroll-finance integration
6. Detect ghost employees or payroll fraud
7. Ensure policy adherence (leave, advances, reimbursements)

---

## SCOPE

### In-Scope Processes
1. **Payroll Processing**: Attendance, calculation, approval, disbursement
2. **Recruitment & Onboarding**: Hiring requisitions, approvals, background verification
3. **Employee Master Data**: Salary changes, transfers, personal data updates
4. **Time & Attendance**: Biometric/timesheet, leave management
5. **Payroll Accounting**: Journal entries, reconciliations
6. **Statutory Compliance**: PF, ESI, TDS on salaries, Professional Tax
7. **Employee Separations**: Resignations, F&F settlements, no-dues clearance
8. **Policy Compliance**: Advances, reimbursements, overtime

### Period Coverage
- Primary: October, November, December 2025
- Year-to-date review where relevant

---

## RISK ASSESSMENT

### High Risk Areas
- **Ghost employees**: Fake employees on payroll
- **Unauthorized salary changes**: Fraudulent salary hikes
- **Statutory non-compliance**: Late PF/ESI/TDS payments (penalties)
- **Incorrect TDS**: Under-deduction leading to company liability
- **F&F settlement errors**: Disputes, legal issues

### Medium Risk Areas
- Attendance manipulation
- Payroll calculation errors
- Delayed salary payments
- Master data integrity

### Low Risk Areas
- Routine monthly payroll for existing employees
- Standard leave processing

## METHODOLOGY

### Phase 1: Planning & Process Understanding (Week 1)

**Activities:**
1. Review Payroll SOP and HR policies
2. Conduct walkthroughs:
   - HR Senior Manager (recruitment, exits)
   - Payroll Accountant (payroll processing)
   - Finance Executive (payroll accounting)
3. Understand payroll system/software
4. Identify key controls and control owners
5. Obtain employee list and organizational structure

**Deliverables:**
- Process understanding memo
- Control identification matrix
- Risk assessment worksheet

### Phase 2: Data Collection (Week 1-2)

**Data Requests:**
1. Payroll registers for Oct, Nov, Dec 2025
2. Employee master list as of Sept 30 and Dec 31, 2025
3. List of new joiners in Q3 (with joining dates)
4. List of exits in Q3 (with exit dates)
5. Attendance reports for Q3
6. Leave records for Q3
7. Salary revision letters/approvals in Q3
8. PF challans and ECR for Oct, Nov, Dec
9. ESI challans for Q3 (if applicable)
10. TDS on salaries - deduction schedules and Form 24Q for Q3
11. Professional Tax challans (if applicable)
12. Bank statements showing salary transfers
13. Payroll journal entries in Tally for Q3
14. HR policies (leave, advance, reimbursement)

**TM1 Support:**
- Extract payroll ledgers from Tally
- Prepare payroll expense trend analysis

**TM2 Support:**
- Review compliance calendar for HR statutory items

### Phase 3: Control Testing (Week 2-3)

#### Test P1: Payroll Register Review and Reconciliation
**Objective**: Ensure payroll registers are complete and accurate

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain payroll registers for Oct, Nov, Dec
2. For each month:
   - Verify employee count matches HR records
   - Check for any unusual names or duplicates
   - Verify total gross pay, deductions, net pay
   - Recalculate totals
   - Compare month-to-month for significant variances
3. Reconcile Q3 payroll with Tally:
   - Total salary expense in GL
   - Total statutory deductions (PF, ESI, TDS)
   - Total net pay vs bank transfers

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-01 to Q3-PAY-03

**Expected Evidence:**
- Payroll registers
- Tally salary expense ledger
- Reconciliation worksheet

#### Test P2: Payroll Calculation Accuracy
**Objective**: Verify payroll calculations are correct

**Procedure:**
1. Select sample of 15 employees:
   - Stratify by department, level, salary range
   - Include at least one from each department
   - Include high earners and threshold employees (PF/ESI)
2. For each employee, recalculate December 2025 payroll:
   - Gross pay (basic, HRA, allowances)
   - PF deduction (12% of basic, subject to ceiling)
   - ESI deduction (if applicable: 0.75% if gross ≤₹21,000)
   - TDS (based on projected annual income and declarations)
   - Other deductions (loans, advances)
   - Net pay
3. Compare recalculation with payslip
4. Investigate any discrepancies

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-04 to Q3-PAY-06

**Expected Evidence:**
- Employee payslips
- Salary structure documents
- TDS computation worksheets
- Recalculation workpapers

#### Test P3: Attendance and Leave Integrity
**Objective**: Ensure attendance data is accurate and approved

**Procedure:**
1. Select 2 months (October and December)
2. For each month:
   - Obtain attendance report
   - Verify manager approval
   - Select 10 employees randomly
   - Cross-check attendance with payroll (days paid)
   - Review leave taken vs leave balance
   - Check for any manual overrides and approvals
3. Test leave management:
   - Verify leave policy
   - Check leave application and approval for 10 instances
   - Verify leave deduction in payroll (if unpaid leave)

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-07 to Q3-PAY-08

**Expected Evidence:**
- Attendance reports with manager sign-off
- Leave applications and approvals
- Leave balance reports

#### Test P4: Payroll Approval and Sign-off
**Objective**: Verify payroll is reviewed and approved before disbursement

**Procedure:**
1. For each month (Oct, Nov, Dec):
   - Verify payroll register has HR Head/Finance Manager sign-off
   - Check approval date (before salary payment date)
   - Verify variance analysis performed (if SOP requires)
   - Check for any unusual items flagged and resolved

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-09

**Expected Evidence:**
- Signed payroll registers
- Approval emails
- Variance analysis (if any)

#### Test P5: Salary Disbursement
**Objective**: Ensure salaries paid correctly and timely

**Procedure:**
1. For each month (Oct, Nov, Dec):
   - Obtain bank statement showing salary transfer
   - Verify bulk transfer date (should be by salary date per policy)
   - Reconcile total transfer amount with net payroll
   - Select 10 employees randomly
   - Trace their net pay from payroll to bank statement
   - Verify payment to employee's registered bank account
2. Check for any failed transfers and resolution

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-10 to Q3-PAY-11

**Expected Evidence:**
- Bank statements
- Salary transfer file/report
- Employee bank account details

#### Test P6: Payroll Master Data Changes
**Objective**: Ensure salary changes are authorized

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain log of all salary changes in Q3
2. For each change:
   - Verify reason (promotion, increment, correction)
   - Check authorization (appraisal letter, promotion letter, HR Head approval)
   - Verify effective date
   - Check Finance Manager review
   - Verify change reflected correctly in payroll

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-12

**Expected Evidence:**
- Salary revision letters
- Appraisal/promotion approvals
- HR Head and Finance Manager sign-offs

#### Test P7: New Hire Onboarding
**Objective**: Verify hiring process and controls

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain list of all new joiners in Q3
2. Test all if <10, else sample 10
3. For each new hire:
   - Verify Hiring Requisition Form (HRF)
   - Check approvals (HOD, MD if required per level)
   - Verify position in approved headcount/AOP
   - Check offer letter issued and accepted
   - Verify background verification completed
   - Check onboarding checklist completed
   - Verify employee created in HRMS/payroll with correct details
   - Check first salary payment (pro-rata if mid-month)

**Workpaper**: Q3-EMP-01 to Q3-EMP-02

**Expected Evidence:**
- Hiring Requisition Forms
- Approval emails
- Offer letters
- Background verification reports
- Onboarding checklists
- HRMS/payroll entries

#### Test P8: Employee Separations and F&F Settlements
**Objective**: Ensure exit process is controlled and settlements accurate

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain list of all exits in Q3
2. Test all if <10, else sample 5
3. For each exit:
   - Verify resignation letter or termination letter
   - Check acceptance by management
   - Verify exit interview conducted (if policy requires)
   - Check no-dues clearance checklist completed
   - Verify company assets returned (laptop, ID, etc.)
   - Recalculate F&F settlement:
     * Salary for worked days
     * Leave encashment (per policy)
     * Gratuity (if >5 years service)
     * Any other dues
     * Less: Notice period recovery (if applicable)
   - Verify Finance/HR senior review of F&F
   - Check F&F payment made timely
   - Verify employee removed from payroll (no salary in subsequent month)

**Workpaper**: Q3-EMP-03 to Q3-EMP-04

**Expected Evidence:**
- Resignation/termination letters
- No-dues clearance checklists
- F&F calculation worksheets
- Approval sign-offs
- F&F payment proof

#### Test P9: Provident Fund (PF) Compliance
**Objective**: Verify PF deductions and deposits are correct and timely

**Procedure:**
1. For sample of 10 employees:
   - Recalculate PF deduction (12% of basic, capped at ₹1,800 if basic >₹15,000)
   - Verify employer contribution (12% or 13% as applicable)
   - Compare with payroll register
2. For each month (Oct, Nov, Dec):
   - Obtain PF challan
   - Verify payment date (should be by 15th of next month)
   - Reconcile challan amount with payroll register
   - Check ECR filing (if available for sample month)
3. Test PF reconciliation (if prepared by company)

**Workpaper**: Q3-HRC-01 to Q3-HRC-02

**Expected Evidence:**
- PF calculation worksheets
- PF challans with payment dates
- ECR filing acknowledgments
- PF reconciliation

#### Test P10: Employee State Insurance (ESI) Compliance
**Objective**: Verify ESI deductions and deposits (if applicable)

**Procedure:**
1. Identify employees with gross pay ≤₹21,000 (ESI threshold)
2. For sample of 5 such employees:
   - Recalculate ESI deduction (0.75% of gross)
   - Verify employer contribution (3.25% of gross)
   - Compare with payroll register
3. For each month (Oct, Nov, Dec):
   - Obtain ESI challan (if applicable)
   - Verify payment date (by 15th of next month)
   - Reconcile challan with payroll
4. Check ESI return filing (if applicable)

**Workpaper**: Q3-HRC-03

**Expected Evidence:**
- ESI calculation worksheets
- ESI challans
- ESI return acknowledgments

#### Test P11: TDS on Salaries
**Objective**: Ensure TDS withheld correctly based on declarations

**Procedure:**
1. For sample of 10 employees (focus on high earners):
   - Obtain employee's investment declaration (Form 12BB or similar)
   - Recalculate annual taxable income
   - Compute annual TDS liability
   - Verify monthly TDS deduction (1/12th or as per projection)
   - Compare with payroll register
2. Verify Form 24Q for Q3 (Oct-Dec):
   - Obtain filed 24Q
   - Reconcile with payroll TDS deductions
   - Check filing date (due by 31st Jan 2026)
   - Verify Senior Accounts Manager review before filing
3. Check TDS deposits:
   - Verify TDS deposited by 7th of next month for Oct, Nov, Dec

**Workpaper**: Q3-HRC-04 to Q3-HRC-06

**Expected Evidence:**
- Employee investment declarations
- TDS computation worksheets
- Form 24Q filed
- TDS challans

#### Test P12: Professional Tax (if applicable)
**Objective**: Verify PT deducted and deposited

**Procedure:**
1. Determine if PT applicable (state-specific)
2. If yes:
   - Verify PT deduction in payroll (per state slab)
   - Check PT payment challans for Q3
   - Verify payment by 15th of each month

**Workpaper**: Q3-HRC-07

**Expected Evidence:**
- PT challans
- Payment dates

#### Test P13: Payroll Accounting and Integration
**Objective**: Ensure payroll entries in books are accurate

**Procedure (TM1 Lead):**
1. For each month (Oct, Nov, Dec):
   - Obtain payroll journal entry from Tally
   - Verify entry components:
     * Dr. Salary Expense (gross pay)
     * Cr. PF Payable (employee + employer)
     * Cr. ESI Payable (employee + employer)
     * Cr. TDS Payable
     * Cr. Other Deductions
     * Cr. Bank (net pay)
   - Reconcile journal entry with payroll register
   - Verify Finance Manager approval of entry
2. Test clearing of payables:
   - Verify PF payable cleared when PF paid
   - Verify ESI payable cleared when ESI paid
   - Verify TDS payable cleared when TDS paid
3. Year-to-date reconciliation (Apr-Dec):
   - Total salary expense in GL
   - Total per payroll registers
   - Reconcile any differences

**Workpaper**: Q3-INT-01 to Q3-INT-03 (TM1)

**Expected Evidence:**
- Tally journal entries
- Payroll registers
- Reconciliation worksheets
- Payable ledgers

#### Test P14: Fraud Detection - Ghost Employees
**Objective**: Detect any ghost employees on payroll

**Procedure:**
1. Obtain employee list from HR (as of Dec 31, 2025)
2. Obtain list of salary bank transfers for December
3. Cross-verify:
   - Any names in bank transfer not in HR list?
   - Any duplicate bank accounts?
   - Any duplicate PANs?
4. Investigate any discrepancies
5. Analytical review:
   - Check for employees with unusually high salaries for their level
   - Check for employees with no attendance but full salary

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-13

**Expected Evidence:**
- Employee list from HR
- Bank transfer list
- Cross-verification worksheet
- Investigation notes

#### Test P15: Policy Compliance - Advances and Reimbursements
**Objective**: Ensure advances and reimbursements follow policy

**Procedure (TM2 Lead):**
1. Obtain list of salary advances in Q3 (if any)
2. For each advance:
   - Verify policy limit compliance
   - Check approval per policy
   - Verify recovery schedule
   - Check recovery in subsequent payrolls
3. Obtain list of reimbursements processed through payroll (if any)
4. For sample of 10 reimbursements:
   - Verify supporting bills/receipts
   - Check manager approval
   - Verify policy compliance (limits, eligible expenses)

**Workpaper**: Q3-POL-01 to Q3-POL-02 (TM2)

**Expected Evidence:**
- Advance request forms and approvals
- Recovery schedules
- Reimbursement claims with bills
- Manager approvals

#### Test P16: Analytical Procedures
**Objective**: Identify anomalies requiring investigation

**Procedure (TM1 Support):**
1. Month-on-month payroll expense analysis (Oct vs Nov vs Dec)
2. Department-wise salary expense
3. Identify significant variances (>10%)
4. Investigate variances:
   - New hires?
   - Exits?
   - Bonuses?
   - Errors?
5. Compare Q3 to Q2 and prior year Q3

**Workpaper**: Q3-PAY-14 (TM1)

**Expected Evidence:**
- Trend analysis charts
- Variance analysis
- Management explanations
