## QUALITY CONTROL PROCESS

### Daily
- Each team member updates status tracker
- Document work in workpapers same day
- Flag critical issues to IA Manager

### Weekly
- Team huddle (progress, challenges)
- IA Manager reviews completed workpapers
- Update management on preliminary findings

### Phase-End
- Peer review of workpapers (cross-review)
- IA Manager comprehensive review
- Findings validation with process owners
- Exit meeting with management

## ESCALATION MATRIX

### Critical (Immediate to IA Manager + CFO)
- Fraud indicators
- Material misstatement in financials
- Significant compliance violations
- System security breaches

### High (Within 24 hours to IA Manager)
- Control failures with financial impact
- Repeated control exceptions
- Statutory deadline misses

### Medium (Weekly meeting discussion)
- Process inefficiencies
- Minor control gaps
- Documentation issues

### Low (Include in final report)
- Best practice recommendations
- Process improvement suggestions
