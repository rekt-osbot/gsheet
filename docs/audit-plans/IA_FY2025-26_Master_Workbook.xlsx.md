# Internal Audit FY2025-26 - Master Workbook Guide
## SNVA TravelTech Pvt. Ltd.

---

## WORKBOOK STRUCTURE OVERVIEW

This master workbook is designed for a 3-member Internal Audit team executing the FY2025-26 audit programme across three phases: H1 Review, Q3 Review, and Q4 Review.

### Team Structure & Roles

**Team Member 1 (TM1) - Senior Auditor - Revenue & Treasury Lead**
- Primary: Revenue/OTC processes, Treasury & Cash Management
- Secondary: MIS Reporting, IFC coordination
- Skills: Strong in revenue recognition, forex, banking operations

**Team Member 2 (TM2) - Senior Auditor - P2P & Taxation Lead**
- Primary: Procure-to-Pay, Vendor Management, Taxation & Compliance
- Secondary: Fixed Assets, Capex management
- Skills: Strong in procurement, tax laws, statutory compliance

**Team Member 3 (TM3) - Auditor - HR & Systems Lead**
- Primary: Payroll & HR processes, Tally ERP analytics
- Secondary: System controls, data analytics support
- Skills: Strong in payroll, HR compliance, IT systems

**IA Manager** - Overall coordination, quality review, reporting, stakeholder management

## PHASE-WISE WORK ALLOCATION

### H1 REVIEW (Oct-Dec 2025) - Audit Period: Apr-Sep 2025

#### Timeline: 8 weeks (Early Oct - Mid Dec 2025)

**Week 1-2: Planning & Process Understanding**
- ALL: Attend kickoff meeting, review SOPs
- TM1: Revenue/OTC walkthrough, Treasury walkthrough
- TM2: P2P walkthrough, Taxation walkthrough
- TM3: Tally system review, data extraction setup
- IA Manager: Finalize scope, risk assessment

**Week 3-6: Fieldwork**

**TM1 Responsibilities:**
1. Revenue & Order-to-Cash (40% effort)
   - Sample 30 sales invoices (BPO + Ticketing) across Apr-Sep
   - Test invoice sequencing and completeness
   - Verify 20 customer receipts and bank reconciliation
   - Test FIRC/BRC for foreign remittances (all instances)
   - Review credit notes/refunds (all instances if <10, else sample 10)
   - Test revenue cut-off for Sept 30, 2025
   - Check unbilled/deferred revenue identification

2. Treasury & Cash Management (30% effort)
   - Test bank reconciliations for Apr, Jun, Sep 2025 (3 months)
   - Sample 15 high-value bank payments for authorization matrix compliance
   - Review FD interest accruals (if any)
   - Test forex remittance handling (5 transactions)
   - Verify cash flow monitoring processes

3. Support Activities (30% effort)
   - Coordinate with Finance Manager for data requests
   - Document control testing in workpapers
   - Weekly status updates

**TM2 Responsibilities:**
1. Procure-to-Pay (40% effort)
   - Sample 25 purchase transactions across expense categories
   - Test 3-way match (PO, GRN, Invoice) for each sample
   - Verify vendor onboarding for all new vendors in H1
   - Test purchase approvals and budget compliance (20 samples)
   - Review vendor master data changes (all instances)
   - Test competitive bidding for purchases >₹50,000 (all instances)
   - Examine AP aging and long-outstanding items

2. Taxation & Compliance (40% effort)
   - Test TDS deduction on 10 payments (professional fees, contracts, rent)
   - Verify Q1 and Q2 TDS returns vs books
   - Test one month's GST return (July 2025) - output and input
   - Review GST input credit reconciliation (GSTR-2B vs books) for 2 months
   - Verify compliance calendar maintenance
   - Check advance tax payments for Q1 and Q2
   - Cursory review of PF/ESI payments (Apr-Sep)

3. Support Activities (20% effort)
   - Vendor file documentation review
   - Tax challan verification
   - Workpaper documentation

**TM3 Responsibilities:**
1. Tally ERP Analytics (50% effort)
   - Extract sales register and verify invoice numbering continuity
   - Extract purchase register and scan for anomalies
   - Review Tally audit trail for H1 period
   - Test period locking controls for each month
   - Verify user access rights and segregation of duties
   - Extract ledgers for analytical review
   - Generate exception reports (weekend entries, round amounts, etc.)

2. Data Analysis Support (30% effort)
   - Prepare revenue trend analysis (monthly)
   - Prepare expense analysis by category
   - Identify outliers and unusual transactions
   - Support TM1 and TM2 with data queries

3. Documentation Support (20% effort)
   - Maintain master workpaper index
   - Ensure all tests are properly documented
   - Prepare data visualization for findings

**Week 7: Completion & Exit Meeting**
- ALL: Complete pending tests, prepare findings summary
- IA Manager: Conduct exit meeting with Finance team

**Week 8: Reporting**
- IA Manager: Draft report
- ALL: Review and provide inputs
- IA Manager: Finalize and issue report

### Q3 REVIEW (Jan-Feb 2026) - Audit Period: Oct-Dec 2025

#### Timeline: 6 weeks (Early Jan - Late Feb 2026)

**Week 1: Planning & Data Collection**
- ALL: Review Q3 scope (Payroll & HR focus)
- TM3: Lead planning meetings with HR and Payroll teams
- TM1 & TM2: Support with financial integration aspects

**Week 2-4: Fieldwork**

**TM3 Responsibilities (PRIMARY LEAD - 70% effort):**
1. Payroll Processing (40% effort)
   - Review payroll registers for Oct, Nov, Dec 2025
   - Recalculate payroll for 15 sample employees (diverse levels/depts)
   - Test attendance data integrity for 2 months
   - Verify payroll approval and sign-offs (all 3 months)
   - Test salary disbursement - trace to bank statements
   - Review payroll master data changes (all instances)
   - Test payroll accounting entries in Tally (all 3 months)
   - Reconcile payroll register with GL

2. Statutory Compliance - HR (30% effort)
   - Recalculate PF for 10 sample employees
   - Verify PF challans for Oct, Nov, Dec (timely payment)
   - Test ESI for employees near threshold (5 samples)
   - Verify ESI challans for Q3
   - Review TDS on salaries for high earners (10 samples)
   - Verify Form 24Q for Q3
   - Check professional tax payments (if applicable)

3. Employee Lifecycle (20% effort)
   - Review all new hires in Q3 (or sample 10 if >10)
   - Check hiring requisitions and approvals
   - Verify background verification completion
   - Review all exits in Q3 (or sample 5 if >5)
   - Test full & final settlement calculations
   - Verify no-dues clearance checklists

4. Fraud Testing (10% effort)
   - Cross-verify employee list with bank transfer list
   - Check for duplicate PANs or bank accounts
   - Analytical review for ghost employees

**TM1 Responsibilities (SUPPORT - 30% effort):**
1. Payroll-Finance Integration (20% effort)
   - Verify payroll journal entries in books
   - Test reconciliation of statutory payables (PF, ESI, TDS)
   - Review year-to-date payroll reconciliation

2. Analytical Review (10% effort)
   - Month-on-month payroll expense analysis
   - Department-wise salary expense trends
   - Identify and investigate anomalies

**TM2 Responsibilities (SUPPORT - 30% effort):**
1. Statutory Compliance Review (20% effort)
   - Review compliance calendar for HR statutory items
   - Verify timely filing of returns
   - Check for any penalties or interest paid

2. Policy Compliance (10% effort)
   - Review salary advances (if any) for policy compliance
   - Check reimbursements for proper documentation
   - Verify leave encashment calculations

**Week 5: Completion & Reporting**
- TM3: Prepare draft findings
- ALL: Review and validate findings
- IA Manager: Conduct exit meeting

**Week 6: Final Reporting**
- IA Manager: Draft and finalize Q3 report
- ALL: Management response facilitation

---

### Q4 REVIEW (Apr-May 2026) - Audit Period: Jan-Mar 2026 + Year-End Close

#### Timeline: 8 weeks (Early Apr - Late May 2026)

**Week 1: Planning & Pre-Close**
- ALL: Coordinate with Finance for year-end close schedule
- TM1: Focus on MIS and closing schedule
- TM2: Focus on Fixed Assets preparation
- TM3: Focus on IFC control matrix preparation

**Week 2-5: Fieldwork (Concurrent with Finance Close)**

**TM2 Responsibilities (PRIMARY LEAD - 40% effort):**
1. Fixed Assets & Capex (30% effort)
   - Review all asset additions in FY25-26 (or sample if >20)
   - Verify capex approvals against AOP
   - Test capitalization vs expense classification (10 samples)
   - Recalculate depreciation for 10 sample assets
   - Review asset disposals (all instances)
   - Test physical verification report (if conducted)
   - Reconcile Fixed Asset Register with GL
   - Review CWIP status (if any)

2. Year-End Reconciliations - AP & Vendors (10% effort)
   - Review AP reconciliations with vendor statements
   - Test balance confirmations (sample 10 major vendors)
   - Review old credit balances

**TM1 Responsibilities (PRIMARY LEAD - 40% effort):**
1. Financial Statement Close Process (25% effort)
   - Verify closing schedule adherence
   - Review balance confirmations sent to customers
   - Test AR reconciliations (sample 10 major customers)
   - Review provisions and accruals (sample 10 significant items)
   - Test unbilled revenue and deferred revenue calculations
   - Verify year-end bank reconciliations (all accounts)
   - Test forex revaluation (if applicable)
   - Review Chart of Accounts management

2. MIS Reporting (10% effort)
   - Verify monthly MIS for Jan, Feb, Mar
   - Test MIS accuracy vs accounting records
   - Review management variance analysis
   - Verify full-year MIS ties to financial statements

3. Manual Journal Entries (5% effort)
   - Review all material manual JEs in Q4
   - Focus on late March and early April adjustments
   - Verify approvals and supporting documentation

**TM3 Responsibilities (PRIMARY LEAD - 40% effort):**
1. IFC Controls Testing (25% effort)
   - Prepare IFC control matrix (all key controls)
   - Summarize H1 and Q3 control test results
   - Test any remaining untested controls
   - Evaluate control deficiencies and remediation
   - Assess fraud risk controls
   - Review IT general controls for Tally
   - Test system access controls and user rights

2. Tally Year-End Controls (10% effort)
   - Verify period locking for all months
   - Review Tally audit trail for year-end adjustments
   - Test books closure and lock for FY25-26
   - Verify backup and data integrity controls

3. Analytical Procedures (5% effort)
   - Full-year financial analysis (YoY, vs budget)
   - Key ratio analysis
   - Identify areas requiring deeper audit focus

**ALL Team Members (20% effort each):**
1. Regulatory Compliance Check
   - TM1: Income tax return preparation status
   - TM2: Tax audit report status (if applicable)
   - TM3: Annual compliance report review

2. Subsequent Events Review
   - Review Apr-May 2026 transactions for FY25-26 impact

**Week 6: Completion & Management Debrief**
- ALL: Complete fieldwork
- IA Manager: Conduct wrap-up meeting with senior management

**Week 7-8: Comprehensive Reporting**
- IA Manager: Draft Q4 and IFC reports
- ALL: Review and finalize
- IA Manager: Issue final reports to Audit Committee

## WORKPAPER STRUCTURE

### Standard Workpaper Format (All Team Members)

Each workpaper should include:
1. **Header**: Audit area, Period, Prepared by, Reviewed by, Date
2. **Objective**: What control/process is being tested
3. **Procedure**: Steps performed
4. **Sample Selection**: Basis and list of samples
5. **Test Results**: Detailed findings for each sample
6. **Exceptions**: Any deviations noted
7. **Conclusion**: Overall assessment
8. **Cross-References**: Links to supporting documents

### Workpaper Indexing System

**H1 Review:**
- H1-REV-01 to 20: Revenue/OTC (TM1)
- H1-TRY-01 to 10: Treasury (TM1)
- H1-P2P-01 to 20: Procure-to-Pay (TM2)
- H1-TAX-01 to 15: Taxation (TM2)
- H1-SYS-01 to 15: Tally/Systems (TM3)
- H1-ANA-01 to 10: Analytics (TM3)

**Q3 Review:**
- Q3-PAY-01 to 25: Payroll (TM3)
- Q3-HRC-01 to 15: HR Compliance (TM3)
- Q3-EMP-01 to 10: Employee Lifecycle (TM3)
- Q3-INT-01 to 05: Payroll Integration (TM1)
- Q3-POL-01 to 05: Policy Compliance (TM2)

**Q4 Review:**
- Q4-FAR-01 to 20: Fixed Assets (TM2)
- Q4-FSC-01 to 25: Financial Close (TM1)
- Q4-MIS-01 to 10: MIS Reporting (TM1)
- Q4-IFC-01 to 30: IFC Testing (TM3)
- Q4-SYS-01 to 10: System Controls (TM3)
- Q4-ANA-01 to 10: Analytics (TM3)

## RISK ESCALATION MATRIX

### Critical (Immediate escalation to IA Manager + CFO)
- Fraud indicators
- Material misstatement in financials
- Significant compliance violations
- System security breaches

### High (Escalation to IA Manager within 24 hours)
- Control failures with financial impact
- Repeated control exceptions
- Statutory deadline misses

### Medium (Document and discuss in weekly meeting)
- Process inefficiencies
- Minor control gaps
- Documentation issues

### Low (Include in final report)
- Best practice recommendations
- Process improvement suggestions

## APPENDICES

### A. Sample Size Guidelines
- **High-risk controls**: 25-30 samples or 100% if <25
- **Medium-risk controls**: 15-20 samples
- **Low-risk controls**: 10-15 samples
- **High-value transactions**: 50% of value or all if <10
- **New processes**: 100% or minimum 10 samples

### B. Interview Guidelines
- Prepare questions in advance
- Document responses contemporaneously
- Obtain interviewee sign-off if critical information
- Follow up on unclear responses

### C. Observation Testing
- Schedule in advance with process owner
- Document date, time, process observed
- Note any deviations from SOP
- Take photos if relevant (with permission)

### D. Data Analytics Techniques
- Benford's Law for fraud detection
- Trend analysis for anomalies
- Ratio analysis for reasonableness
- Duplicate detection
- Gap analysis (sequence testing)
